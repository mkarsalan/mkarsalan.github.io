Kahaniyan

Mobile Assisted Second Language Learning Application

In Urdu, Kahaniyan means STORIES. Developed this Android application using Unity3D and Fungus (a visual scripting tool), to explore the use of gamification on non-native primary school children and to assist language learning within the context of interactive storytelling.
